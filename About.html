About Clay & Cactus

A local market created to bring small businesses, makers, and the community together.

For shoppers, Clay & Cactus Market is meant to feel easy to explore and full of local goods that are thoughtful, original, and made with care. We want people to come expecting a market that feels personal, welcoming, and rooted in community.

For vendors, the intention is to create a space where small business owners and makers can show their work proudly, meet new customers, and be part of a market that values creativity and local connection.

At its core, this market is about giving the community a place to discover local talent while giving vendors a setting that feels supportive, inviting, and worth being part of.
