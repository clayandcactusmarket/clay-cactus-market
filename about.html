About Clay & Cactus

Clay & Cactus Market is a curated community market built to connect Cadence with handmade, artist-made goods.

Clay & Cactus Market was created to meet a clear local need: a dedicated space in Cadence where residents can discover thoughtful, original work from artists, makers, and small businesses.

Founded by Jaymi, an artist working across portraits, ceramics, and graphic design, the market is shaped by both creative experience and practical leadership. With a background in sales and logistics, Jaymi brings the organization and strategy needed to build a strong, community-first market.

Founded and led by Jaymi, the market is currently in its launch phase. Creative direction, social media, event planning, and vendor curation are being managed with a focus on building a strong, long-term foundation for both shoppers and vendors.

The current focus is final location approval and active review of incoming vendor applications. Once location approval is finalized, vendor selections will be shared and official market dates, participating vendors, and event details will be announced.
